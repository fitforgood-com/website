Exercise is essential

We all know that exercise is essential for living well into old age, but it’s hard to find a way to make it part of daily life.

Fit for Good is a convenient, enjoyable way to help you improve their fitness.

By participating in two fun classes a week, each followed by a half hour social break, you can increase your strength, stability and balance.
